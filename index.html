Information

We are New Jersey State Roleplay, a community based on the game Emergency Response: Liberty County within ROBLOX. We offer enhanced and fun roleplay experience within our server sessions. Join our Discord.

Why should I play NJRP?

New Jersey State Roleplay aims for realism and professionalism within our server.

- We are one of the largest and most active ERLC communities. We offer custom liveries, daily server startups, professional & trained staff team, variety of departments, active discord server, booster perks, events, giveaways, and more!

- Sessions will be hosted at their scheduled times daily. The server will stay up as long as Staff are available to Moderate.

Departments

We have many departments, which all offer extra roleplay opportunities. In each department, you will have the opportunity to apply, gain knowledge, and traverse your way through the many ranks offered to you. Join our Department Hub.

  • Trenton Police Department

    Trenton Police Department's goal is to maintain a stable functioning community within Trenton. They vow to protect Trenton and its community. They offer a variety of subdivisions as-well as high quality and realistic roleplay.

    Apply Here!

  • Special Weapons and Tactics

    New Jersey Special Weapons and Tactics is a tactical police unit tasked with responding to high-risk felony calls, including hostage situations, active shooters and calls that local police simply cannot handle.

    Discord

    Apply Here!

  • Mercer County Sheriffs Office

    Here at Mercer County Sheriff's Office, we strive to be able to become one of the best Law Enforcement agencies out there! If you are looking to become a patrol officer in the county, you've stopped at a great place.

    Discord

    Apply Here!

  • Trenton Fire Department

    Trenton Fire and Rescue aim to provide medical care, and fire rescue services. Their goal is to ensure the safety and well-being of Trenton's residents at all times.

    Apply Here!

  • Department of Transportation

    Welcome to the Department of Transportation. We offer a variety of ranks and hard workers who strive to help others out within the community. If you like to change tires, tow cars, and more... apply today!
