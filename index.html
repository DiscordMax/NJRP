About Us

New Jersey State Roleplay aims for realism and professionalism within our server. We are one of the largest and most active ERLC communities. We offer custom liveries, daily server startups, professional & trained staff team, variety of departments, active discord server, booster perks, events, giveaways, and more! Discord.
